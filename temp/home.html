Who Can Use Safe.Spaza?

Owners

Manage inventory and receive alerts for expiring products

Manufacturers

Register products and monitor for counterfeits

Authorities

Monitor compliance and receive alerts about unsafe products

Inventory Management

Keep track of your stock levels, receive alerts for low stock, and manage product expiration dates efficiently.

Barcode Scanning

Scan product barcodes to verify authenticity and check product information instantly.

Counterfeit Detection

Identify potentially counterfeit products to protect your customers and business.

Real-time Reporting

Generate reports on inventory, sales trends, and expired products for better decision-making.
